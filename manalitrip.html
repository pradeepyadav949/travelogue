Mesmerising Manali Trip Package From Chandigarh


2 Nights 3 Days Package

Overview

Manali is one of the most visited hill stations in the North Indian state of Himachal Pradesh, and an ideal destination for all types of tourists. Mostly visited by honeymooners and adventure enthusiasts, Manali pampers its visitors with exceptional views of magnificent mountains, luscious pine and cedar forests and views of gorgeous meadows with far reaching carpets of flowers. Booking a Manali tour package from Chandigarh and other nearby major cities, beforehand would surely save your time and let you enjoy a hassle free vacation.

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival, Shopping, Local sightseeing

Commence your 3 days Manali trip from Chandigarh as you reach Manali today.

Today, as you reach Manali, you will be transferred to the hotel, with the help of one of our trusted agents who will receive you at the railway station/bus station. Now as you reach the hotel, freshen up and get ready to embark on an exciting and thrilling Manali sightseeing tour.

As you start the tour, you will visit the sacred Hadimba Temple with your loved ones. Hadimba Temple is a religious hub for the locals there and an important sight for the tourists. Dedicated to Goddess Hadimba, you will fall in love with the location where the temple is situated. Surrounded by luscious pine and cedar forests and lofty mountains, the temple certainly offers scenic and photographic views. Later in the day, also visit places like Van Vihar, Vashisht Baths, The Great Himalayan National Park, Manu Temple, Nehru Kund etc. And when it's time to wind up the day, you can explore the lively markets of Manali. These markets offer a wide variety of handmade products for the vacationers to take back home as souvenirs.

End the day by heading back to hotel. Enjoy a delicious dinner and an overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 2: Rohtang Pass, Solang Valley

Second day of this amazing Manali holiday package from Chandigarh will take you to Rohtang Pass/Solang Valley

A day trip to Rohtang Pass is as essential as visiting Hadimba Temple while in Manali. Rohtang Pass is the snow point where tourists traveling to Manali flock in to enjoy in the snow and engage in activities like horse riding, skiing, paragliding, zorbing, trekking etc. As you begin the day with a scrumptious breakfast, embark upon an enthralling 52 kilometre journey to Rohtang Pass from Manali.

Rohtang Pass is a prime location for shooting purposes also apart from just being a tourist attraction. Movies like ‘Jab We Met’ and ‘Yeh Jawani Hai Deewani’ have been shot here. With the mounting heights, you will witness more serene landscapes which will certainly leave you wonderstruck.

However, the visit to Rohtang Pass is subject to availability and in that case you will be taken to Solang Valley which is as glorious as Rohtang Pass.

After an exciting day, return to the hotel in the evening for dinner and overnight stay.

Day 3: Trip ends - Departure

This short and crisp Chandigarh to Manali vacation package comes to an end today.

It's time to pack your bags and bid a goodbye to Manali. After a lovely breakfast, get ready to leave the hotel and proceed towards the railway station/bus station to start your journey onward.
