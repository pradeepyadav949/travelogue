Bali 5 Days Honeymoon Package

Overview

Home to achingly beautiful beaches lined with palm trees and crystal clear waters, Bali is one of the 17,000 Indonesian Islands. Famous for not just it's sun and sand, this island offers a journey through endless experiences dousing one in the authentic Balinese culture and lifestyle. Couples from world over head to Bali to explore the historical treasures like temples, rice terraces, volcanoes and numerous monuments and markets to make their honeymoon a success. With these Bali honeymoon itinerary 5 days, newlyweds can enjoy the culture, cuisine, markets, sightseeing, adventure activities, watersports, luxury and much more that Bali has to offer at reasonable prices.

Bali honeymoon itinerary 5 days lets travellers enjoy sheer luxury and refreshment as they experience the lavish stays, private transfers, hassle free commuting, full day adventure tours, jungle safaris, underwater walks, dinner cruises and some of the best beaches of Asia. Do not forget to explore the grooving nightlife and shopping scenes in Bali and have the much needed fun at Bali Safari and Marine Park as well. This Bali honeymoon itinerary for 5 days begins with the first day in Kuta at leisure followed by the Bounty Dinner Cruise at night with live music and performances. The second day is filled with adventure as you visit the natural wonders of Bali including the GitGit Waterfall, Twin lakes of Bali as well as a tour of the water temples here. Head to Nusa Dua on the third day for exploring the surroundings, enjoying watersports and shopping or opt for a tour of the beautiful beaches of South Bali. The fourth day is at leisure for you to explore and relax at your private villa as per your desire. On the last day of your Bali honeymoon itinerary 5 days, bid farewell to the charming yet soothing island of Bali as you check out from the villa get transferred to the Denpasar international airport to head back home with loving recollections of your romantic honeymoon.

Itinerary

Day 1

Bali: Arrival and bounty cruise

Feel warmly welcomed with a marvelous dinner cruise as per your Bali honeymoon itinerary 5 days!

On reaching the Denpasar International Airport, get greeted by our agent’s representatives and then get transferred to your pre booked hotel in a private car. After checking in, relax for the day and unwind as this time is at leisure for you. Head for the Bounty Dinner Cruise in the evening to experience the sparkling romance and love with your sweetheart. Climb aboard this luxurious cruise to feast on a lavish dinner, serenading live music, traditional Bali dance and a cabaret show. Afterwards, head back to the hotel to your beds for a cozy sleep.

Day 2

Kuta: Full Day Sightseeing Tour - Gitgit waterfall, Twin Lake

Witness the charms that will captivate your heart on the second day of this Bali honeymoon itinerary!

Rise to a delicious buffet breakfast at the hotel and then get ready for an adventure filled day. Begin by visiting the famous Gitgit waterfall, one of the most visited picturesque waterfall of Bali followed by the crystal clear bliss-pool at the bottom here. Afterwards, walk among the aromatic paddy fields and clove plantations. You can also head for hiking in the Story Gitgit and Campuhan areas. Make your way to Bali's third biggest lake, Lake Buyan and Lake Tamblingan popularly called the Twin Lakes of Bali.

Now you will witness the Water temples of Bali. Visit Pura Ulun danu Bratan temple, situated beside the lake Bratan, followed by the famous Pura Taman Ayun with its marvelous architecture, vast courtyards and gardens with lotus and fish ponds. Finish the tour at Tanah Lot temple and then return to hotel for a comfortable overnight stay.

Day 3

Nusa Dua: Sightseeing - Beaches, Watersports

Explore the sun kissed beaches of South Bali or head to Nusa Dua for fun times with your 5 days Bali honeymoon tour!

Post a scrumptious breakfast, check out from the hotel. Head for a fun filled tour of the lively Nusa Dua to make your honeymoon memorable. Begin by indulging in some thrilling water sports at Tanjung Benoa followed by a delicious lunch (to be paid for directly). Indulge in shopping at Bali collections, Nusa dua and visit the sacred Uluwatu temple. Check in to the private pool villa for a relaxing time. You can also opt to watch the Kecak and Fire Dance in Nusa Dua. Head out for some shopping and lounging at the Kuta Beach with markets like Krishna and Ramayana nearby.

You can also choose to experience the vibrant half-day tour of South Bali Beaches. Visit famous beaches like Bali Pandawa Beach in Kutuh Village and the hidden Dreamland Beach. Travel by a private car and stop wherever your heart desires. Head to a shack for some food and groovy music at the Canggu Beach in La Briza. Return to the hotel for a warm sleep at night.

Day 4

Nusa Dua: Leisure day - Pool Villa, Go shopping

Relax and spend quality moments with your loved one with this Bali honeymoon itinerary 5 days!

After a mouthwatering breakfast at the hotel, you are free to spend this day with your loved one as you wish. Enjoy leisurely as you lounge by the pool or go out exploring the unique culture, cuisine and lifestyle of Bali. Head out for some shopping in the evening and make your way to the beaches for a perfect end to the day. Head back to hotel afterwards and enjoy a sound night’s sleep.

Day 5

Departure

Say goodbye to Bali on the last day of this 5 days Bali honeymoon!

After a flavorful breakfast at the hotel, check out and get transferred to the Denpasar International Airport. On the way, you can also stop for some last minute shopping. Board your flight back home.
